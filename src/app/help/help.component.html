How to Select a Game and Category

  1. Choose Your Game:

    On the main page, you will see multiple game options displayed as cards. Each card represents a different game with its name and description.

  2. Select a Game:

    Click on the card of the game you wish to play. This will open a dialog box where you can choose a category for the selected game.

  3. Choose Your Category:

    In the dialog box, you will see the title "Choose Your Category." Below the title, you will see the name of the selected game. Click on the dropdown menu to view and select a category from the list.

  4. Check Category Details:

    Once you select a category, details such as the number of words and the last update date will be displayed.

  5. Start the Game:

    Click the "Let's Play" button to start the game. You will be redirected to the game page where you can begin playing.

Messy words

Goal: Rearrange the scrambled letters to form a correct English word.

How to Play: Click the letters to change their order until you form a correct word.

Sort words

Goal: Sort the words in the correct order, such as alphabetically or by specific categories.

How to Play: Drag the words or click on them to arrange them according to the given criteria.
